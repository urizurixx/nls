FAQ

Have a question?

If you can't find the answer to your question here, please email us at Info@nextlevelsuccessevents.com

How Do I Register For An Event?

Please visit the Register for Events tab, click here, or call: (800) 361-0756

What If I Have A Problem Registering Online?

Please email: info@nextlevelsuccessevents.com and you will receive instructions to resolve your question or be registered manually.

May I Bring A Guest?

Yes. Your admission includes registration for up to four guests. Please select “Register a Guest” and provide the full name and email address for each guest. You may also register a guest when you receive your confirmation email. Your guest will receive a confirmation email at the address provided.

Do I need my ticket to attend?

No. If you have completed the registration process, you are confirmed and we have reserved a seat for you.

How long is the event?

The event usually lasts 2 – 3 hours depending on audience questions. Doors typically open 15 minutes prior to the start of the event.

May I reschedule my event?

Yes. If there is another date or venue in your local area that better suits your schedule, please email:  info@nextlevelsuccessevents.com or call: (800) 361-0756 so we know when to expect you.

Will there be any refreshments?

No refreshments are provided.

May I bring my children?

We discourage bringing children to the event so that you, and other program participants, are able to get the most out of the session.

What is the attire?

Business or business casual attire.

I don't see an event listed
for my city; are you coming to my area?

Stay in touch with Daymond John’s Next Level Success on Facebook for the latest information on upcoming events.

Will Daymond John be at the event?

Daymond will not attend this particular event, however a trusted member of Daymond’s entrepreneurial team will be presenting his strategies on his behalf.

Is parking free?

Parking is determined by the hotel venue.

What can I expect to learn at the preview event?

The program covers various business strategies and techniques. For example, business structures, funding, marketing, and sales techniques.

Will I be sold anything?

The session is free of charge. Daymond John’s Next Level Success reference materials will be available at the back of the room once the event is over; however, the purchase of any materials is not required.

Why is the pricing discounted so steeply at your live events?

We offer significant discounts at our live event because it costs much less to train one-to-many than one-on-one. For that reason, nearly 100% of our sales are made at live events and we would encourage you to attend one of our live events before purchasing any of our courses. If you would like to order at single retail rates, however, please feel free to do so by calling our Customer Service retail sales number: 800-771-6558.

Would you place me on the do not contact list?

Please call our Customer Service department at (800) 206-0429 for assistance.

Where can i hear what others have to say about Daymond John's Success Formula?

Please tune in to Daymond John’s Next Level Success on YouTube for student testimonials.

Are Daymond John's Next Level Success Events a Scam?

No. Daymond John’s Next Level Success was founded on the principles and strategies Daymond John has learned through his extremely successful career. Next Level Success has a simple goal — to help others achieve financial freedom like Daymond John through education, hard work, and determination.
