4 Simple Ways to Join the Sharing Economy

Thu Nov 10 2016

GetAround is an app that lets people rent cars near them. It’s a lot like services like Zipcar, but instead of renting from a company with a fleet of vehicles, users can rent from other individual users. If your car stays parked most of the time, and you know when you won’t need it, you can make money by letting others rent it for a few hours at a time with GetAround.

Or, if you don’t own a car or you’re traveling and don’t want to rent a car for the whole trip, you can rent one through GetAround and save a lot of money while only using the car when you need it.

CLEAN OUT YOUR CLOSET AND MAKE MONEY WITH POSHMARK 

With Poshmark, women can buy and sell gently used designer clothing and accessories. If you’re like a lot of women, and you find yourself donating a ton of barely worn clothing every season, you’ll enjoy making some cash instead.

INSTACART 

Being a part of the shared economy isn’t just about finding new ways to make money. It’s also about finding more convenient ways to live your life. If you’re busy driving for Uber, creating products for your Etsy store, and doing other work to build a small business, you can easily run out of time to go to the grocery store. Fortunately, with Instacart, you can pay a reasonable markup and delivery fee to get someone else to do your shopping for you and deliver your groceries to your home. Or, if you have time and you want a new source of income, you can become an Instacart shopper and make deliveries for cash.

SPINLISTER 

If you’re an avid cyclist, you know what it’s like to go on vacation somewhere with gorgeous on- and off-road cycling opportunities. Unfortunately, taking your bike with you is often unfeasible or, at the very least, expensive. And the rentals you find at shops really don’t cut it. What if you could rent bikes, skis, snowboards, and other sporting equipment from other avid sports enthusiasts?

With Spinlister, you can. Or you can let other people rent your equipment when they’re in your area. This app is a great way to make some cash, make vacations less expensive and more fun, and even do some networking with other athletes. You never know what you might find out when you get to talking with a fellow mountain biker or skier.

These are just a few of the many simple and easy ways to join the movement and become a part of the sharing economy. Remember, the sharing model can help you whether you’re short on time and need help with daily tasks or you’re in the market to make some money helping others. Literally anyone can benefit from participating in this new economic model, and it can give you a lot more financial freedom and independence than your day job. Look into these and other mobile apps that can help you change the future of work and take full advantage of the sharing economy.
