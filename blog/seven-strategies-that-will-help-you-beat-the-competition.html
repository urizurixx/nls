Seven Strategies That Will Help You Beat the Competition

Wed Nov 18 2015

You will always have competition. And, likely, your competition will grow as more and more people start their own businesses. Thanks to the Internet, someone could start a company on the other side of the country and compete with you. With that in mind, you have to set the goal of always finding a way to be a step ahead of the competition. Here are seven tips that I have put together so you can do just that.

VERY CLEARLY CREATE AND DEFINE YOUR BRAND

You have to have a brand, and it must be clearly defined. Your brand represents everything about your business. It’s not just a logo or a color scheme. Instead, it is everything that defines your business: the image you present, the products you sell, your customer service, your business ideals, and yes, your logo and your catchphrases. If it has to do with your business, it is part of the brand. Make sure people recognize it right away. The more you strengthen your brand and raise brand awareness, the better for your business.

FIND A WAY TO BUILD A COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE

In order to beat the competition, you have to have an advantage over them. Offer something that no one else does. Find a unique take on things. Find something that makes you stand out so that people have a reason to do business with you and not with the competition.

TREAT YOUR CUSTOMERS LIKE FAMILY

People want a different type of customer experience these days. They want to be treated as if they are the only person that matters. If you don’t offer that, your clients will go somewhere else. Anyone who does business with you should be treated with professionalism and courtesy, and they should get a personalized experience.

DON’T TALK AT YOUR CUSTOMERS

Too many people find themselves talking at the customers: offering a sales pitch, pushing a product, explaining how to use a product, and so on. The problem is, people don’t like to be talked at. Instead, talk with your customers. Have real conversations. Let them talk. You will learn what they want and need so that you can ensure your business fulfills that need.

OFFER EXCITING THINGS

You need to get people excited about your business so that they come to you and no one else. Offer something exciting like a free gift or an amazing sale. Drum up excitement to keep people coming through the door.

OFFER A COMPELLING REASON TO CHOOSE YOU

Your company has to be compelling. You have to come across as the very best opportunity for a customer. They have to have a reason to choose you. This could include the best customer service, the better product, the better price, or something else.

PROVIDE A REAL SOLUTION TO A PROBLEM

All businesses should fulfill a need. And yours does in some way or another. Just make sure your solution is the very best way to fulfill that need. When a potential customer looks at you and the competition, they should see that you offer the better solution so that they will want to do business with you. People will go to the business that offers a true solution to whatever need they are trying to fulfill.

These seven tips will help you stand out from the competition and gain the advantage you need to be successful.
