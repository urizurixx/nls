Top 4 Reasons Personal Branding is Essential

Mon Sep 05 2016

All too often, entrepreneurs and small business owners overlook the importance of personal branding. They think that their company’s brand is enough and that personal branding is just for people who want to get famous. The thing is, though, personal branding really isn’t just for people who want to become “gurus” in their field or get reality TV shows about their businesses. It’s actually an essential part of building any entrepreneurial effort.

Whether you want your name to be synonymous with the quality products and services that your business provides or you want to be known for making savvy entrepreneurial moves to start successful business after successful business, people need to be able to recognize you and your personal brand. Not convinced yet? Let’s take a look at just a few of the top reasons that personal branding is essential for your business.

PEOPLE LIKE DOING BUSINESS WITH PEOPLE

Want to earn your customers’ trust? Directly connect your face and name to your business. Show them that you’re a real, live human being with a story and a dream. Build your personal brand, and they’ll feel like they’re doing business with you, not a nameless, faceless company.

DEVELOPING YOUR PERSONAL BRAND WILL MAKE YOUR MORE CONFIDENT 

Not only is personal branding good for your business, but it’s also good for you personally. Building your personal brand requires you to look at your strengths, your drive, your inspirations, and what sets you apart. When you look at these things, you’ll recognize that you bring a lot to the table and that you may actually be much more qualified to be a business leader than you once thought.

Building that confidence with your personal brand will in turn make you more confident when you talk with investors and new clients. They’ll notice that you really believe in yourself and your brand, and it’ll have a positive effect on how they see your business’ brand, as well.

YOUR EMPLOYEES CAN BUILD YOUR BUSINESS WITH THEIR OWN PERSONAL BRANDING

Personal branding isn’t just about you, though. It’s also about your employees. If you create an empowered and energetic work environment, you’ll also be empowering your employees to represent your business with their own personal brands.

Encourage them to be themselves (but also to be professional) as they represent your brand on social media, and your target audience will see that you have quality people working for you with their own brands, as well. This will further build trust and brand loyalty with new and returning customers.

GET MORE IMPRESSIONS ACROSS SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORMS

If you use your own personal brand and encourage your employees to use their personal brands, then you’ll have the potential to reach thousands more potential customers, investors, and other relevant people each day. Why? Because you and your employees each have hundreds or thousands of followers across different social media platforms. If each of you uses your personal brand to represent your business, then you’ll raise awareness significantly in very short order.

Personal branding helps you build yourself into your business. It helps others see that you’re serious about your goals and that you’re doing everything you need to do to succeed. Personal branding is also valuable if you’re a business owner and you want to spread the word about your brand through your own social media activity and your employees’ social media posts and shares. The more you take advantage of personal branding, the more it can do for you and your business.
