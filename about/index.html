About Daymond John

DAYMOND JOHN is a pioneer entrepreneur, CEO, award-winning businessman, founder of multi-billion-dollar brand FUBU and a Shark on ABC’s Shark Tank. Daymond’s marketing strategies and ability to build successful brands has made him a highly influential and sought-after consultant and motivational speaker today.

In 2009, he joined the cast of ABC’s Emmy Award-winning business show Shark Tank, which is now in its 11th season.

Daymond has authored multiple books including the New York Times and Wall Street Journal best-sellers, The Power of Broke and, his most recent book, Rise and Grind, which deconstructs the daily habits and rituals of some of the world’s most successful entrepreneurs, executives, musicians, TV personalities, and more. Daymond is also the author of Display of Power and The Brand Within.
